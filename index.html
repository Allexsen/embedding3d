    What is this?

    The cloud is English words from GloVe (50-dimensional vectors), projected to 3D with UMAP. Tiers from 500 to 400,000 words — default 20K, switchable top-right. Position is meaning: related words cluster.

    Search & arithmetic: type a word to light up its nearest neighbors, or do vector math — king − man + woman, paris − france + italy. Expressions draw a hop-by-hop path you can scrub with the jumper bar.

    What gets downloaded

    Only the word tier you pick: the default 20K tier is ~4 MB, bigger tiers more (progress is shown while loading). Everything runs in your browser; nothing you type is sent anywhere.

    More

    The sentences → page does the same for whole phrasings — plus sampling an LLM to show wording changes what a model says.
